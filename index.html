🌿 Vision

To be India’s most respected and forward-thinking environmental advisory firm, setting benchmarks in regulatory excellence, sustainability leadership, and responsible industrial transformation. We aspire to create a future where businesses grow with confidence, guided by robust environmental governance, seamless compliance, and a deep commitment to ecological balance.

🌱 Mission

At Harveda Enviro Solutions, our mission is to deliver strategic, end-to-end environmental and regulatory solutions that enable industries to operate responsibly, efficiently, and in full compliance with evolving legal frameworks. To provide expert-driven, integrated services across Environmental Clearance (EC), EPR, CGWA, and Pollution Control compliances with authorities such as Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Central Pollution Control Board, Gujarat Pollution Control Board, and Central Ground Water Authority. To combine regulatory expertise with practical industry insight, ensuring timely approvals, risk mitigation, and operational continuity for our clients. To uphold the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and transparency in every engagement. To drive sustainable industrial growth by embedding environmental responsibility into business strategy and decision-making. To act as a long-term strategic partner, delivering value through innovation, compliance excellence, and client-centric solutions.
